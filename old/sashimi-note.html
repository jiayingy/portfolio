Sashimi Note allow users to create and edit documents using markdown language. It can also transform the files to documents and slides view.

How did Sashimi Note come about?

As students, we find that it was hard to annotate on the documents provided by lecturers which were often in PDF format converted from PowerPoint slides. Annotations with a PDF viewer is often restrictive and not flexible.

So, we thought of why not have a document creating and editing platform which allows both lecturers and students to have full editing capabilities and allowing lecturers to easily convert from one format to another by just maintaining one file!

What I did?

Through interviews with lecturers in school and after coming up with the user needs, I sketched out the UI Design I had in mind on paper.
